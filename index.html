Example: “How do I pay road transport tax for a sedan?”

Answer:

To pay road transport tax for a sedan in Botswana, visit your nearest Department of Road Transport and Safety (DRTS) office or use the government e-services platform. Bring your vehicle registration, national ID, and proof of insurance. Fees vary depending on engine size. Payment can be made via card or cash.
